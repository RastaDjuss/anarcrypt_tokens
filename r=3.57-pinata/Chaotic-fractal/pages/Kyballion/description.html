General Description

Published in 1908 under the pseudonym "Three Initiates", The Kybalion is a text aimed at transmitting ancient Hermetic teachings while highlighting seven fundamental principles that govern the workings of the universe.

Mental Transmutation

A key process in The Kybalion, mental transmutation is the art of transforming one's thoughts and emotions. According to the text, this mastery allows access to a more balanced and harmonious life.
