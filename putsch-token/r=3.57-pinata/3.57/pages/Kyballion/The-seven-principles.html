The Seven Hermetic Principles

Each principle from the Kybalion represents a universal law governing the spiritual, mental, and physical worlds. Discover their relevance and implications in everyday life.

1. Mentalism

"The All is Mind; the Universe is Mental."

The Principle of Mentalism explains that all reality originates from the Universal Mind.

2. Correspondence

"As above, so below; as below, so above."

The Principle asserts a constant correspondence between the spiritual, mental, and physical planes.

3. Vibration

"Nothing rests; everything moves; everything vibrates."

This principle states that everything in the universe is in constant motion, manifesting as vibrations at varying frequencies.

4. Polarity

"Everything is dual; everything has poles; everything has its pair of opposites."

This principle explains the duality inherent in all things and how opposites are merely extremes of the same essence.

5. Rhythm

"Everything flows, out and in; everything has its tides; all things rise and fall."

This principle describes the natural ebb and flow of energy and phenomena in the universe.

6. Cause and Effect

"Every cause has its effect; every effect has its cause."

The Principle of Cause and Effect explains how every action produces consequences, creating a chain of events.

7. Gender

"Gender is in everything; everything has its Masculine and Feminine Principles."

This principle emphasizes the dual masculine and feminine forces that operate across all aspects of existence.
